Festival Electro-House

25-26-27 September 2020

Sportpaleis Antwerpen

Information Event

For this first edition, the Elecro-House Festival will take place at the Antwerp SportsPalais from 25 to 27 September 2020 with 56 hours of non-stop mixing and 10 of the greatest DJs of the moment.

This edition promises to be breathtaking both by its music and by the play of lights and the decor of the stage.

With its profits, the festival aims to support actions to protect nature and endangered animal species and actions to fight against the abandonment of animals. The names of the first five artists were unveiled this Saturday. To tell us about this new appointment, we welcome Armin Van Buuren, Hardwell ans many more.
